What is Naturopathy?

Naturopathy is a system of man building in harmony with the constructive principles of Nature on physical, mental, moral and spiritual planes of living. It has great health promotive, disease preventive and curative as well as restorative potential. According to the manifesto of British Naturopathic Association, "Naturopathy is a system of treatment which recognizes the existence of the vital curative force within the body." It therefore, advocates aiding human system to remove the cause of disease i.e. toxins by expelling the unwanted and unused matters from human body for curing diseases.

Why Naturopathy

Much of today’s illnesses are chronic in nature and build up over a long period of time from multiple causes. Many of the principles of naturopathy involve stripping away these harmful influences and allowing the body to live in true health, creating prevention medicine so the body operates in optimum health. Naturopathic medicine treats the patient from the preventive stage through to serious, chronic and debilitating disease. Therefore, people can go to Naturopaths for colds, bronchitis, allergies, as well as for heart disease, diabetes, and malignant diseases – Prevention is the best cure.
